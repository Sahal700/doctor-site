I am an accomplished Consultant Psychiatrist currently serving at Almas Hospital, Kottakkal. After completing my MBBS from AIIMS, Delhi, I pursued an MD in Psychiatry at NIMHANS, Bangalore, followed by a DNB in Psychiatry. My professional journey includes serving as a Junior Resident at NIMHANS for three years, followed by a year as a Senior Resident at Manjeri Medical College. I also gained valuable experience in the departments of Cardiology and Laboratory Medicine at AIIMS, Delhi.
Fluent in Malayalam, Hindi, English, Kannada, and Tamil, I strive to make mental healthcare accessible to people from diverse backgrounds. I am a proud member of the Indian Psychiatric Society (IPS) and the Indian Medical Association (IMA), committed to upholding the highest standards of clinical excellence.
